Speaker submissions

Have a talk you'd like to give at the Chi Hack Night? Email Derek!

Chi Hack Night presentations are 15 minutes or less in length, with an open Q & A session after. Presenters can be any government agency, non-profit, company or group who've made use of open government data or built a civic technology application.

The Chi Hack Night audience consists of a diverse group of 80-100 web developers, designers, academic researchers, data journalists, activists, policy wonks and curious citizens.

A few rules of thumb for a good presentation:

  • Make it technical and/or wonky
  • Don’t spare us the details
  • Show, don’t tell
  • Be honest about what worked and what didn’t
  • Don’t try to sell us your product or agenda
  • Attend a Chi Hack Night prior to your talk and get a feel for the community
