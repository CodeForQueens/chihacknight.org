Discussion

Between hack nights, we use the internets for coordinating and collaborating on civic technology projects. Here's how you can get connected:

Google Group

Our primary mode of online communication is the chihacknight Google Group, shown below (if your browser is cool with external iframes).

Twitter

On Twitter, we use the #chihacknight hashtag.

Subscribe to event updates

Every week, we send out an email and update this site to preview the next Open Gov Hack Night presentation. Sign up for email updates or subscribe to our RSS feed.

Smart Chicago Blog

Smart Chicago Collaborative records and writes about most Open Gov Hack Night presentations on their blog.

Civic Tech Voices

Cathy Deng runs a communal blog called Civic Tech Voices, with the goal of amplifying more diverse perspectives and stories in civic tech.
