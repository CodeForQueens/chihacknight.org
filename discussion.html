Discussion

Between hack nights, we use a bunch of different tools for coordinating and collaborating on civic technology projects. Here's how you can get connected:

Open Gov Hack Night Google Group

Our primary forum for discussion is the chihacknight Google Group. This group is for general announcements and collaboration on projects started at the Open Gov Hack Night.

Civic Tech Voices

The offical blog of the Open Gov Hack Night! A communal blog with the goal of amplifying more diverse perspectives and stories in Chicago civic tech. Anyone can contribute - just contact the site's editor, Cathy Deng.

#chihacknight on Twitter

On Twitter, we use the #chihacknight hashtag to organize tweets about the Open Gov Hack Night.

Subscribe to event updates

Every week, we send out an email and update this site to preview the next Open Gov Hack Night presentation. You can also subscribe to our events RSS feed.

Smart Chicago Blog

Smart Chicago Collaborative records and writes about most Open Gov Hack Night presentations on their blog.
