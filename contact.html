Contact

The Chi Hack Night is organized by Derek Eder, Christopher Whitaker and (formerly) Juan-Pablo Velez.

Derek Eder
Founder and Lead Organizer

Derek is a web developer with a passion for open government, open source and open data. He is Founder and Parter at DataMade, and Co-Founder of Open City.

Christopher Whitaker
Organizer and Documenter

Christopher runs the civic technology consulting firm CivicWhitaker, consults for the Smart Chicago Collaborative and is the Midwest Brigade Coordinator for Code for Ameria.

Juan-Pablo Velez (emeritus)
Founder and Organizer

Juan-Pablo is a data analyst and civic technologist. He is a leader and collaborator at Open City and Co-Founder of the Data Science for Social Good Summer Fellowship.
