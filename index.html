Chi Hack Night is Chicago's weekly event to build, share, and learn about civic tech.

Join a group of passionate folks working at the intersection of open government, cities, and technology. This is an evening of civic tech building, sharing, learning and networking.


The Chi Hack Night is for those who want to:

  • learn about civic tech, open data and open government
  • get work done on civic projects
  • start a new project, or find one to join (non-coders very welcome!)
  • bounce ideas and get help from tech and gov experts

There's always food, too!

This video from The Grid on Gaper's Block will give you a sense of what to expect.

Partners

The Chi Hack Night is organized by members of Open City, hosted at Braintree and documented by Smart Chicago Collaborative.
